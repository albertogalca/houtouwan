World / Santiago de Compostela / Biblioteca y Archivo de Galicia

It is located in Santiago de Compostela, Galicia. In the north of Spain. It was designed by the American architect Peter Eisenman and forms, together with six other buildings, the City of Culture of Galicia. Its shapes simulate the geology of the area. Keep in mind that it doesn't have many outlets due to its architecture.
