World / Seoul / Cafe Sanare

Bukhan Mountain, better known as Bukhansan, has one of the best hiking trails in Seoul. Every year, countless tourists and locals visit the mountain for a challenging hike. Cafe Sanare is located at the foot of Bukhansan, and its serenity makes it a great spot to unwind after a long day of hiking. The interior of the cafe is furnished in a mid-century modern style, with rattan furniture, neutral-coloured cushions, and metal tables with transparent glass tops.
