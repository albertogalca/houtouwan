World / Bangkok / Piccolo Vicolo Café

Piccolo Vicolo is a coffee shop that forms part of Galile Oasis, an arts and community hub and boutique hotel in the regenerated Ratchathewi neighborhood, which also houses a gallery and theatre. It is tucked down Wat Phraya Yang alley, hence the name, which translates from Italian to ‘small alley’. The open space is filled with verdant plants and vintage furniture for a laidback, laptop-friendly setting, where freelancers will feel right at home. They have plugs, good wifi and good coffee for good price (100 baths for Americano).
