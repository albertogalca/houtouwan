World / Bangkok / JEDI Café & Bar

It's located in the Old Town of Bangkok. It is a quiet, picturesque space with white walls and good coffee. They have a small terrace where you can read or enjoy a drink, which directly overlooks the canal. JEDI is a mutual ground for all thinkers run by creative individuals with high complexity through fresh robust brewery, arts, food and spirits, located in the heart of Bangkok. They often have exhibitions and artworks or paintings by artists.
