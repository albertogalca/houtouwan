World / Madrid / La Casa Encendida

La Casa Encendida is social and cultural center located in the Lavapiés neighbourhood. The center has a library, youth library, media library and office room, as well as a cafeteria. The Terrace of La Casa Encendida is an extraordinary place that houses a wide variety of plant species and allows you to contemplate the best sunsets in the city. It is home to the Terrace coffee bar, open all year round, with exquisite breakfasts, brunches, and an extensive list of wines, cocktails and beers. It is located in a neo-Mudejar style building that has a monumental character, designed by the architect Fernando Arbós and inaugurated in 1913. Coffee Shop is located in floor 1 and terrace coffee bar in floor 0.
