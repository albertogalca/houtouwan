World / Barcelona / Norai Raval

Norai Raval is the restaurant of Museu Marítim de Barcelona located in the Ciutat Vella neighbourhood. By enjoying the Norai restaurant you will also be contributing to the Norai project, a social and training project that every year allows access to the labor market for people from the Raval neighborhood in vulnerable situations. It is a very bright space thanks to its large window that overlooks the port and it’s big and open concept.
