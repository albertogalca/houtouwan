World / Barcelona / Les Filles Cafè

It is located in the Gràcia neighborhood (Barcelona). Actually, it's not a cafeteria, it's a restaurant, but outside of peak lunch or dinner hours, you can go to work without problems. It has an interior part and an exterior part. The patio is full of plants and this piece of nature within the city is very pleasant. The coffee is ok.
