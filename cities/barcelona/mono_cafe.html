World / Barcelona / Mono Café

It is located in the Poblenou neighborhood. It is a clear, spacious space with lots of light. It has large windows, good coffee and delicious lemonades without sugar. In addition, it has a small outdoor space as a quiet zone to concentrate. The chairs are not very comfortable. They are stools. It has plugs and good wifi.
