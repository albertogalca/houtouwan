World / Barcelona / Convento de San Agustín

It is located in the Ciutat Vella neighborhood. This cafeteria is located in the Convent of San Agustín, a Gothic building from the 14th century, and has a terrace overlooking the cloister that transmits tranquility and meditation. On Thursday and Friday afternoons they play live music, and it is a highly recommended place. It is a very nice and quiet place away from the hustle and bustle of the city.
