World / Barcelona / La Central del Raval

Located in the Raval neighborhood, Bar Central is located in a green garden like a beautiful patio, full of plants and in an environment that inspires you to be creative. It is inside the La Central bookstore. And it was inaugurated on Sant Jordi (a very beautiful and important holiday for Catalonia. Its peculiar Book Day). Located next to the space formerly occupied by the 'Capella de la Misericòrdia' and with a surface area of ​​850 m².
