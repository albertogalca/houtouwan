World / Taipei / Eighty-Eightea Rinbansyo

This is the typical place we wouldn't go to work. In our case we went to read and write, and it was very calm. The tea is very good, and the atmosphere is very relaxing. You can pour yourself water, they refill your tea when you finish it and you can spend a few hours there enjoying the atmosphere. The place is beautiful and worth it.
